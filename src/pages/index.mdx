# Welcome to Xilos

Xilos is an intelligent agentic AI infrastructure platform by [Mill Pond Research](https://www.millpondresearch.com) that observes, secures, and orchestrates agentic AI across enterprise networks. It operates as a middleware gateway that intercepts, analyzes, and processes every prompt before it reaches an LLM destination.

> **Info:** **New to Xilos?** Start with the [Quickstart](/getting-started/quickstart) — connect your first agent in under 5 minutes. If it speaks the OpenAI API, it works with Xilos.

## About Mill Pond Research

Mill Pond Research is an AI infrastructure company building the unified operational and governance layer for agentic AI. The company combines two products:

- **WorkBench** — Prototype, test, and deploy agents faster with unified access to major AI models, specialized agents, and customizable tools.
- **Xilos** — Observe, secure, and orchestrate autonomous AI systems with real-time governance, intent-based routing, and a private knowledge base.

Together, WorkBench and Xilos help organizations deploy, secure, orchestrate, and control their entire agentic AI ecosystem — maximizing efficiency and innovation while retaining the visibility and control needed to run AI securely at enterprise scale.

## The Three Pillars

Xilos is built on three foundational pillars:

- **[Observe](/platform/observe)** — Total visibility into all AI traffic. Every query, every model, every dollar — tracked in real time.
- **[Secure](/platform/secure)** — Local guardrails, PII detection, prompt injection defense, and policy enforcement — all running before any model is called.
- **[Orchestrate](/platform/orchestrate)** — Semantic caching, multi-model workflows, context compression, and a context engine that compounds knowledge.

## Key Differentiators

- **OpenAI-compatible** — If your tool speaks the OpenAI API, it works with Xilos. One base URL change.
- **Semantic caching** — Reuses responses for semantically similar queries. Reduces cost and latency by 50-90%.
- **Context compression** — Reduces token count by 50-90% before queries reach the LLM.
- **Intelligent routing** — Route queries to the right model based on cost, latency, or capability. Define rules in natural language — no code required.
- **Agent guardrails** — Local PII detection, jailbreak prevention, and content filters. No data leaves your infrastructure.
- **Context Engine** — Every answered query becomes a searchable knowledge asset. Your agents' collective experience compounds.
- **Three deployment options** — Managed SaaS, Private Cloud (VPC), and On-Premise (air-gapped capable).
- **Audit and compliance** — Complete immutable trail of every AI interaction. Built for compliance and forensics.
